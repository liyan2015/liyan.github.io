Research Interests

My research addresses the issue of bridging extreme-scale urban sensor infrastructure, effective data analysis and system designing for complex urban systems in an interdisciplinary perspective. Currently, I am especially interested in the following topics:

  • Data-driven Urban Cyber-Physical Systems

  • Urban Big Data Analytics

  • Mobile Computer Networks
