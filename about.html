About

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher in Senseable City Lab at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I earned my Compute Science Ph.D. degree from the University of Virginia under the supervision of Professor Haiying Shen. Before that, I received my M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Florida in 2013, and my B.E. degree in Information Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2010.
